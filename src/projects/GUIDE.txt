```plaintext
Quick guide: supported directives and short examples

Features
- Headings: `/section Title` and `/subsection Title`
- Ordered lists: `/list` ... `/endlist` (indent with tab or 2 spaces for nesting)
- Unordered lists: `/bullet` ... `/endbullet` (same nesting rules)
- Tables: `/table` ... `/endtable` (first row is header, use `|` to separate cells)
- Images: `/image <URL> [options]` (options comma-separated)
- Videos: `/video <URL> [caption text]`
- Captions: `/caption Text[, italic]` — placed beneath the immediately preceding image if present
- Comments: `%your note` (ignored by renderer)

Directive quick reference
- /section Project Title
- /subsection Overview
  A short project summary.

Lists
/list
Item one
\tSub-item A
Item two
/endlist

or

/list
Item one
    Sub-item A
        Sub-sub-item A
Item two
/endlist

Images and captions
- Basic image:
  /image /aristo.png

- Image with options (aspect, height in px, crop, fit):
  /image /aristo.png aspect=16:9, height=240, crop=top, fit=cover
  /caption A concise caption for the image

- No-crop image fit:
  /image /aristo.png aspect=16:9, fit=contain

- Caption with commas or italic:
  /caption "Caption, with comma", italic

Video
- /video /demo/video.mp4 Short demo description

Tables
/table
Metric | Joint-Position Control | Direct Torque Control
Stability | Jittery and high-frequency vibrations | Smooth and fine-grained force-closure
Payload Capacity | Fails at 0.09 kg | Success up to 0.15 kg (+67%)
Grasp Quality | Limited to form-closure | Achieves stable force-closure
/endtable

Notes
- Relative media paths like `./img.png` are resolved to `/img.png` (public root).
- Keep `/caption` on the line immediately after the `/image` to attach it to that image.
- `fit=contain` renders the image at natural proportion (ignores `aspect`) to avoid letterbox blank space.
- For tables, include at least one row after `/table`; the first row is treated as column headers.
- Lines starting with `%` are treated as author comments and do not render.
- Plain lines become paragraphs.

End of guide
```
